About Prime Impex

Established in 2010, Prime Impex has grown to become a trusted name in the global tiles and sanitary ware trade. With our headquarters in India, we've successfully exported to over 25 countries across Asia, Africa, and the Middle East.

Our mission is to provide high-quality building materials at competitive prices while maintaining the highest standards of customer service and ethical business practices.

We work directly with manufacturers to ensure quality control and cost efficiency, passing the savings on to our valued customers.
